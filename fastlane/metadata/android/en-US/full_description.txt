Simmo picks the right SIM for every outgoing call, automatically, based on the country you're calling. Android's built-in dual-SIM support stops at "ask every time" or "always use SIM X" — Simmo adds simple rules on top: US numbers go out on T-Mobile, European numbers on Vodafone, everything else follows your defaults.

• Rules by destination country — ordered top to bottom, first match wins. Match single countries or whole groups (EU/EEA, North America, Caribbean +1).
• Hand off to another app — send calls to Google Voice or another calling app instead of a SIM.
• Disabled eSIM help — if a rule needs an eSIM that's currently off, Simmo guides you to re-enable it and offers to place the call once it's active.
• No surprises — if no rule matches, your call goes through exactly as it would have. Emergency calls are never touched.
• Private by design — rules and number parsing run entirely on your device. Simmo has no servers, and your dialed numbers are never sent to us.

Works with the system's call redirection role — Simmo is not a dialer and never draws in-call UI. Designed for dual-SIM and multi-eSIM phones like the Pixel.
